It is an ecommerce android application that sells products related to spritual,rituals,puja and other kind of ceremonu.
Features:
	1.welcome screen
	2.authentication(login,signin)
	3.swiper for offer
	4.cart
	5.search filter
	6.profile management(change profile photo,password,etc)
	7.esewa payment
	8.admin dashboard
		a.add,remove product
		b.add,remove offer
		c.update quantity
